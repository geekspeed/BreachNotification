We are writing to you because of a recent event that resulted in the compromise of your personal
information.

about you related to your workers compensation claim may have been contained in the email
bank, including your name, address, phone number, social security number, date of birth, and/or
workers compensation claim number.
In the course of handling your claim for workers compensation benefits, State Fund must at times
share your information with others, such as doctors and interpreters. We are working with
providers to improve how they protect your personal information that they keep on their systems.

that it will take you additional time and effort to monitor your personal information. We are
providing you with the information below about steps you can take to protect your personal
information. We are also offering one year of credit monitoring services at no cost to you.

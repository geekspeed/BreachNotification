SUBJECT: SECURITY INCIDENT NOTIFICATION

Dear IHSS Provider:

We are writing to inform you of a security incident involving your personal information relating to
the In Home Supportive Services program (IHSS). A package containing your personal
information was in transit between the Hewlett Packard (HP) processing center and the State
Compensation Insurance Fund (SCIF). On May 1, 2012, the package arrived, was noted as
damaged, and some of the contents were determined missing. The State is continuing to work
with HP and the SCIF to recover the information, but, to date, it has not been recovered.
